Have you activated CRBT before?

CRBT (Caller Ring Back Tone) is a service on Ethio Telecom that plays music when someone calls you.

If this is your first time, you need to activate it first.

🔵 Step 1 of 2 — Activate CRBT Service
Send "A" to 822 to enable CRBT on your number
To (Recipient) 822
Message A
📱 Tap to Open SMS App & Send

Your SMS app opens with 822 and A already filled.
Just tap Send then come back here.
